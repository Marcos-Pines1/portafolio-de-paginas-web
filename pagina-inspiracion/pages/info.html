RX 6900 XT MASTER 16G

Gaming Revolutionized

The AORUS Radeon™ RX 6900 XT MASTER 16G redefines gaming with its unrivaled performance. Experience seamless gameplay and immersive visuals like never before.

Performance

The Radeon™ RX 6900 XT has 5120 stream processors, new and powerful compute units, and the new AMD Infinity Cache, along with up to 16 GB of dedicated GDDR6 memory, delivering ultra-high frame rates and serious 4K gaming performance.

The metal backplate not only provides an aesthetic form, but also enhances the structure of the graphics card for comprehensive protection.

The silent mode will provide a very muted sound experience with its fans. Changing modes requires a restart to take effect. The default mode is OC.

When experiencing any anomaly in the power supply, the indicator will alert gamers with a flashing light.

Optimal performance and efficiency, ensuring your system stays cool even under the most demanding conditions.
